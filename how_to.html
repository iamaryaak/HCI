How to Use

Simply enter or copy/paste a block of text into the text box and hit next to start scaffolding the text! You have the option to...

  1. Lower every student's readling level by a specific level

  2. Keep every student's reading level at their current level

  3. Raise every student's reading level by a specific level

Why?

Scafholding text means to break up the learning into managable chunks for students. Teachers manage classrooms with students that have varying learning levels. This is especially important in Special Education classrooms, where learning levels are extremely varied. We provide a method using Machine Learning which saves teachers time that they would've lost if they manually scaffolded their curriculum. By using Natural Language Processing (NLP), we change inputted text to different reading levels.
